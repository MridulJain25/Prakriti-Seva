PROJECT TREES

Around 15 BILLION trees are cut down each year!

Trees are EVERYTHING!

Trees not only reduce the CO2 level but also help provide a number of products which are important in our day to day life. If there are more trees the environment quality is enhanced automatically, resulting in better health of citizens

That's why we're doing anything and everything possible to build an organisation that would benefit the environment.

How we do it

All the money generated by the donations for the trees is used by us to buy saplings and plant them in various areas, or we use it to buy chemicals to fight plant diseases.

The team uses the domestic waste created and then turns it into manures and provides it to different farmers, or add it in the soil of plants with different deficiencies.

Our team trimonthly organises different streetplays and campaigns in different areas to motivate people to protect the environment by adopting different methods or joining us.

We also go to different societies and houses for donations and explain people the prevailing evil in the world through streetplays, speeches and many more ways and asking them for their help.

How you can help

On your special occasions you can join us, by giving an amount of money annually on the same date, so that you can help us by coming on that day by planting, watering trees with the help of your money.

You can join us whenever you are free even if it is for half hour or a day weekly.

GIVEAWAYS!!!

If you are a Nature Lover, you can earn 2 free plants that you can sow for the benefit of society.

→ All you need to do to is persuade 5 people to come and work with us for an hour daily for 7 days!
