PROJECT WATER

1 in 4 people in the world lack access to clean water

Water: A Force for Good

We not only use water for domestic purposes, but also help maintain the aquatic ecosystem which are important for the balance of the environment. If there are more water bodies the more beautiful the environment looks and provide more water for advancement.

That's why we're doing anything and everything possible to build an organisation that would benefit the environment.

How we do it

All the money generated by donations for the water is used by us to provide safe drinking water in different areas for lifetime.

Our team goes on in different stagnant water bodies to clean them.

We even have helped different societies to build a Rainwater Harvesting System.

We have helped schools build proper water facilities so that students can study properly.

Our team trimonthly organises different streetplays and campaigns in different areas to motivate people to protect the environment by adopting different methods or joining us.

We also go to different societies and houses for donations and explain people the prevailing evil in the world and asking them for their help.

How you can help

On your special occasions you can join us, by giving an amount of money annually on the same date, so that you can help the society by providing them water facilities.

You can join us and help cean our precious water bodies.

You can join us whenever you are free even if it is for half hour or a day weekly.
