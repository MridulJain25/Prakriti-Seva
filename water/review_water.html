The Face of Clean Water

A Dream come True!

"My family now doesn't worry about water" Manabendra said that his family members had to walk long distances to fetch water, but now they don't have to worry. His family had to also survive with dirty water, but now everything has changed.

A changed life.. through clean water

"Now my children don't need to go to school without water." Sarojini said that everyone in her family had to go without water wherever they had to, but no with clean water facilities they don't have to worry. Her children don't have to suffer in school and can now focus on studies.
